The peptide shows greater GH secretion responses than GHRH in obese patients, whose impaired responses are reversible with weight loss, and operates primarily through hypothalamic mechanisms distinct from GHRH's mechanism of action. ### Key Studies #### Acute dexamethasone administration enhances GH responsiveness to GH releasing peptide-6 (GHRP-6) in man. Micić D, Kendereski A, Popović V et al. · Clin Endocrinol (Oxf) (1996) [](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/8959075/) Cohort n=38 Obese PCOS patients showed impaired GH response to GHRH but normal response to GHRP-6, indicating functional obesity-related defect. #### Growth hormone secretion elicited by GHRH, GHRP-6 or GHRH plus GHRP-6 in patients with microprolactinoma and macroprolactinoma before and after bromocriptine therapy. Popovic V, Simic M, Ilic L et al. · Clin Endocrinol (Oxf) (1998) [](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/9509075/) Cohort n=33 6-24 months GH responsiveness to GHRP-6 recovers in macroprolactinoma after bromocriptine treatment. #### Blocked growth hormone-releasing peptide (GHRP-6)-induced GH secretion and absence of the synergic action of GHRP-6 plus GH-releasing hormone in patients with hypothalamopituitary disconnection: evidence that GHRP-6 main action is exerted at the hypothalamic level. Popovic V, Damjanovic S, Micic D et al. · J Clin Endocrinol Metab (1995) [](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/7883854/) Cohort n=23 GHRP-6 action is primarily hypothalamic; GH secretion blocked in hypothalamopituitary disconnection but preserved for GHRH. #### Diagnosis of growth hormone deficiency in adults by testing with GHRP-6 alone or in combination with GHRH: comparison with the insulin tolerance test. Petersenn S, Jung R, Beil FU · Eur J Endocrinol (2002) [](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/11980622/) Cohort n=20 GHRP-6 alone or combined with GHRH is as sensitive as insulin tolerance test for diagnosing adult GH deficiency.
