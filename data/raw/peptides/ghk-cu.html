Key findings from available studies include tissue repair effects, anti-inflammatory activity in lung disease models, wound healing acceleration in mice, and potential mitigation of smoking-induced muscle dysfunction in cell models. ### Key Studies #### Effects of topical copper tripeptide complex on CO2 laser-resurfaced skin. Miller TR, Wagner JD, Baack BR et al. · Arch Facial Plast Surg (2006) [](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16847171/) RCT n=13 12 weeks GHK-Cu offered no significant reduction in erythema or wrinkles but improved patient satisfaction. #### Tripeptide-copper complex GHK-Cu (II) transiently improved healing outcome in a rat model of ACL reconstruction. Fu SC, Cheuk YC, Chiu WY et al. · J Orthop Res (2015) [](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/25731775/) Animal n=72 12 weeks GHK-Cu improved knee laxity at 6 weeks post-ACLR in rats but effects did not persist. #### Glycyl-l-histidyl-l-lysine-Cu(2+) rescues cigarette smoking-induced skeletal muscle dysfunction via a sirtuin 1-dependent pathway. Deng M, Zhang Q, Yan L et al. · J Cachexia Sarcopenia Muscle (2023) [](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/36905132/) Animal n=20 GHK-Cu rescues cigarette smoking-induced skeletal muscle dysfunction via SIRT1 activation in mice and human cell models. #### Golgi-targeted copper delivery strategy via enhancing copper-dependent proteins' activity for fascia regeneration. Wang R, Xu Y, Saiding Q et al. · J Control Release (2026) [](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41371501/) Animal Golgi-targeted copper delivery enhanced LOX activity 1.78-fold and promoted fascia regeneration in rabbit models. #### The glycyl-l-histidyl-l-lysine-Cu(2+) tripeptide complex attenuates lung inflammation and fibrosis in silicosis by targeting peroxiredoxin 6. Bian Y, Deng M, Liu J et al. · Redox Biol (2024) [](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38879894/) Animal GHK-Cu attenuates lung inflammation and fibrosis in silicosis by targeting peroxiredoxin 6 and reducing oxidative stress.
